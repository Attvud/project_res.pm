Courier CloudWebsite for the aggregator of courier delivery services

Detailed information

On this site, courier service owners can manage their customers, couriers, orders, etc. The service is designed as flexibly as possible in order to be able to expand and modify the functionality in the shortest possible time and with minimal labor costs.

During the development process, the project was modified many times. But this never led to a change in the architectural solutions I chose at the stage of creating the interface. As a result, the client saved more than a dozen hours of programmers' work.

The interface was designed so that the mobile version could be developed in record time.

As a result, my client received a working mobile version of the service on the second day after the developers finished working on the desktop version. I consider this my outstanding achievement.

Scope of the project

  • 120 screens for the desktop version
  • 80 screens for mobile version

For this client, I also made a website for accepting orders from courier delivery clients. And he also developed mobile applications for couriers (iOS, Android). All these products were combined into a single ecosystem. These projects are protected by a non-disclosure agreement (NDA).
