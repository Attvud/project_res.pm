Bysky.byWebsite and two mobile applications for the first private airline in Belarus

Detailed information

Watch in: InVision

For this project, I also did animations:

  • clouds on the home page are moving
  • depending on the time of day of users, the color scheme changed

I decided to make the animation simple enough so that it does not interfere with users, but at the same time, it makes the design unique and attracts attention.

In the process of work, I suggested that the client use a block with the dynamic generation of discounts for private flights. The user had to "catch" his discount.

This idea was accepted.

In the result of my work, the conversion of the site increased by 140%.
