Belpharmprom Website for the largest pharmaceutical holding in Belarus

Detailed information

Watch in: InVision

The holding manages many pharmaceutical factories. Unfortunately, the customer did not have high-quality photographs of all his enterprises.

To smooth out this flaw, I made all the photos in black and white. As a result, chaos in user perception was avoided.

Also, a rather non-standard form of the enterprise slider was chosen. Purpose: to highlight the client's site and make it memorable.
