TRTE.ruOnline store for an official dealer of leading tool and machine-tool factories

Detailed information

Watch in: InVision

From my point of view, this is a reference example of building an interface for an online store. The solution can be easily expanded, modified and adapted to any screens.

Implemented endless loading of relevant products in all lists.

The catalog is available on any page and is made as versatile as possible.

I used an emotional design. The goal is to evoke emotions in users, which always leads to better memorization of the site.

At my request, the customer added an experimental function. Displaying video reviews on all product cards. Reviews could be selected manually or automatically through the Youtube API, based on the name of the product.

The presence of video reviews significantly affected the time spent on the site, conversions and, as a result, the site's position in search engines.
